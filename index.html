Welcome to Powder.ly!

Who Are We?

Powder.ly is a platform connecting independent ski instructors with learners, offering a personalized and affordable alternative to resort-based lessons. We believe in creating opportunities for instructors to thrive and for learners to experience high-quality, tailored instruction.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower independent ski instructors, providing them with tools for autonomy and success while offering learners accessible, high-quality instruction. We envision a community where ski education is democratized, making learning more affordable and rewarding for everyone.

Our Story

Born from a passion for skiing and recognizing the challenges independent instructors face, Powder.ly was created to help instructors thrive without the limitations of traditional resort employment. We want to support the ski community by bridging the gap between instructors and learners, making skiing accessible to all.

Roadmap

Phase 1

Building our instructor network by reaching out to passionate ski instructors and establishing a strong community base.

Phase 2

Launching beta testing to refine the platform, gather user feedback, and enhance the user experience.

Phase 3

Expanding community outreach, adding new features, and partnering with local ski resorts and businesses.

Instructors

Join Powder.ly to gain visibility, financial independence, and be part of a supportive community. We provide the tools you need to manage your own schedule, connect with learners, and grow your business.

Students
