About Us

Learn more about Powder.ly and our mission to connect passionate skiers and snowboarders with local instructors.

Our Mission

At Powder.ly, our mission is to provide skiers and snowboarders with the best possible mountain experience by connecting them with knowledgeable local instructors. We believe that the best way to explore a mountain is with someone who knows it inside and out.

Our Story

Powder.ly was founded by Caleb Forestal, a passionate skier who wanted to create a platform where skiers and snowboarders could easily find and connect with local instructors. With a deep love for the mountains and a vision to make the skiing experience more enjoyable and accessible, Caleb and his team have worked tirelessly to build a community of dedicated instructors and enthusiastic skiers.
